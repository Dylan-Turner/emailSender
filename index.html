Why Use Form Sender?


Form sender lets you send emails hassal free. If your using a static website, don't know php or have lots of different contact forms you can use form sender to send them all. Just send your form to our URL and we will do the rest, no PHP or javascript required on your side! Form Spree is perfect for static websites which can't support PHP so just send it to us.

To use Form Sender you must sign up using the sign up button in the menu. You must sign up as it helps us to reduce any abuse of this service. As we have an obligation to ensure that no spam email is sent from our servers we require that you have to sign up using the email you would like to send emails to. Also to prevent any spam we keep the emails you send if your on a free plan and if they are deemed spam your account will be terminated.

Basic Usage

Notice
Form sender is currently in its early stages, everything works although the documentation is currently limited. Further customisation of the forms and other features are comming soon.

To use Form Sender simply make your HTML form with as many elements as you want. You can include inputs, textareas, selects and more. Once you have created your HTML form you need to add two hidden inputs, one for the email adress you would like the form to go to and one for the page that you want to re-direct to after the form has been successfully submitted. You then need to ensure that the form's action is to the correct adress.

Example:

  • 1. Create your form using any form tags that you want
  • 2. Set the form's action to the correct link (https://formsender.000webhostapp.com/server.php)
  • 3. Add two inputs, one for the EMAILTO and one for the GOTO, ensure that you put the correct details in
  • 4. Test your email out and see if it works
                <form action="https://formsender.000webhostapp.com/server.php" method="POST" id="form">

                  <input class="hidden" type="text" name="EMAILTO" value="ex@mple.com">
                  <input class="hidden" type="text" name="GOTO" value="https://dylanturner.tech">

                  <input type="text" name="name" required>
                  <input type="text" name="email" required>
                  <textarea name="comments" rows="5" cols="50"></textarea>

                  <input type="submit" value="Submit results" id="submitButton">

                </form>

You can have as many inputs as you want. If you are interested in more advanced usage, go see the documentation.

Features
  • Email Limit - The amount of emails you can send daily
  • Email Archive - Your form submissions may be archived
  • Invisible Emails - You no longer require to put your email in the EMAILTO part of the form. Instead you will be given a bunch of strings which will act as your email adress. This results in increased privacy and your email won't be visible to people inspecting your site
  • Custom instance - A custom instance would involve this software being deployed on your own server so that you are not using the same server adress as everyone else. This would result in quicker email times as well as increased security
